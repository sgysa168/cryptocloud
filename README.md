## What Is CryptoCloud, Actually?

CryptoCloud is a cryptocurrency payment gateway — basically, it sits between your customer (who wants to pay in BTC or USDT) and your business (which wants to receive funds). The platform handles all the blockchain complexity: generating invoices, tracking confirmations, and crediting your balance.

It works for online stores, digital product sellers, Telegram bots, subscription platforms, donation pages — really anything where someone might want to pay you in crypto without you having to babysit the blockchain.

Supported currencies include BTC, ETH, LTC, USDT, USDC, TUSD, TRX, BNB, TON, DOGE, SHIB, SOL, and more. The list keeps growing.

## Features Worth Actually Caring About

**Auto-conversion to USDT**
Worried about receiving BTC and watching its value drop overnight? CryptoCloud can automatically convert incoming payments to USDT stablecoin. The platform picks the best rate from 10+ licensed operators. Turn it on in project settings, no integration changes needed.

**Static Wallets**
Instead of generating a new address per invoice, you can assign permanent wallet addresses to customers. Good for subscription setups or repeat buyers — funds credit automatically when received.

**AML Transaction Checks**
Every transaction can be automatically screened. High-risk transactions get flagged and stopped before they land in your account. This protects you from accidentally receiving "dirty" crypto, which can cause problems on exchanges later. Activate it in project settings — takes about thirty seconds.

**Auto-Withdrawal**
Set a schedule (daily, weekly) or a threshold balance, and CryptoCloud automatically moves funds to your external wallet. Withdrawal addresses are protected by 2FA. Users report funds arriving within 30 minutes.

**WalletConnect Support**
Customers using MetaMask, Trust Wallet, or similar can confirm payments directly from their wallet app — no manual address copying. Currently supported on Ethereum and Binance Smart Chain.

**Customizable Checkout**
Add your company logo. Choose the commission payer. Set payment accuracy thresholds (so if a customer pays $99.20 on a $100 invoice, it still confirms). Checkout page supports 7 languages.

**Multi-Project Dashboard**
Manage multiple businesses from one account. Invite team members with role-based access. Export transaction history to spreadsheet for accounting.
